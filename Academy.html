Admission for Academy is open throughout the year, Admission is open for players age of 4 years to 16 Years old. We run player development program for 12 months which helps to develop your child Tactically, Technically, Physically and Mentally. Our Licenced and Experienced coaches focus on writing the best session plans for players of every age group. We follow coaching methodologies that include the overall development of the child in a particular age group. The players are trained in their particular age group in the academy we have U8, U10, U12, U14 & U16. We believe that your child not only learns great football at our academy but we also focus on moral values that will make your child a good and responsible citizen of our country. Our Aim is the create future stars and magical players that will represent national level football and help our country to achieve more than before.
